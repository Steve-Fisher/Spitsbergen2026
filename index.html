Spitsbergen 2026

A rough plan & route.

  • Estimated cost £1500-£2000 per person.
    • Flights UK to Longyearbyen currently showing at £500
    • Boat Longyearbyen to Billefjorden rtn ? £400
    • Group costs (sea freight, gun hire, satphone, etc) c.£300 per person
    • Food (sea freighted + in Longyearbyen) c. £300
    • Personal Gear (skis, tents, cookers, etc) ???
  • Boat drop at Scottehytta (regular tourist boat).
  • Few days "aclimitising" & exploring. Fossil Ridge day walk. Tarantallen day walk. Load carry to top of Ragnarbreen.
  • Full day-walk to camp on nunatak in middle of Mittag-Lefflerbreen (used in JUSE and High Places trips)
  • Long day-walk up Formidablebreen to nunatak at top (used in JUSE 93)
  • Few day-trips from camp. Big snow-capped ski-mountains to east. Stretch objective if excellent weather and everyone up for it would be Newtontoppen.
  • Two day ski south to Fleming Fjellet
  • Few days camped below Fleming Fjellet, climbing it and De Geer Fjellet
  • Day walk back to Scottehytta

Rough Route

Scottehytta

Where we finished JUSE in 1993, and the boat landing spot for High Places treks from in 1999 & 2000.

Ragnarbreen

Walk left-to-righ up this (600m at top) to access Mittag-Lefflerbreen

Tarantallen

A day walk

Walking across the Mittag-Lefflerbreen

Dry glacier all-the-way. Might be lumpy in places, but hopefully OK for pulk-dragging

Mittag-Lefflerbreen Camp

Used in 1993, 1999 and 2000. Right in the middle of the 6km wide glacier, with uninterrupted views north

Walking up the Formidablebreen

Day glacier all-the-way. It will be a long day to reach camp, but better a long day than trying to camp on dry glacier.

Camp at top of Formidablebreen

Used in 1993. A bit rocky, but better than camping on ice. Stunning view!

Mountains next to Formidablebreen Camp

Several nearby options for mountains from camp. Should be possible to ski up & down (or crampon up and ski down).

Mountains accessible from Formidablebreen Camp

Skiing along the top of a ridge of snow covered mountains

Mountains accessible from Formidablebreen Camp

Wouldn't complain about seeing this view again! Mountain top is about 1400m

View of Fleming and De Geer Fjellet

  • From Formidablebreen Camp back would be new to me. A 2 day route looping a bit east, then south means staying around 1000m, which should be snow all the way.
  • Fleming Fjellet is prominent from everywhere. Hopefully we can climb it (might involve some low-grade snow climbing).
  • De Geer Fjellet has a prominent position above a the massive Nordenskiold Breen glacier (1000m below it to the south).
  • From a Fleming Fjellet camp, it would be downhill all the way to Scottehytta. The glacier heading that way is small and from all arial pictures it looks like a straight-forward route.
